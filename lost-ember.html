A Lost Ember review!

Lost Ember is a game developed by mooneye studios, in the game you play as a wild beast called Kalani as you explore the world to help an ember get to the city of light.

The story is heartbreaking as you uncover the mystery of you life before you became a wild beast, as you explore you uncover more and more about your backstory until you finally reach the city of light at the end of the game.

As for gameplay, as you travel you uncover that you can transform into other creatures as a way to traverse the world easier. This discovery takes you through burrows, mountains and even the sky!

The soundtrack and enviorment design is also a phenomenal part of the game as it truly allows the player to feel immersed within the game world aswell as the characters.
