Minecraft is a game developed by Mojang studios and is available across many platforms such as console and pc.

This game does not have a defined story, although it does have advancements to help the player discover many game play elements such as the end dimension.

This is a sandbox game that focuses on the creativity of the player to progress, allowing for the creation of many buildings and farms.

There are many structures availabe for the player to discover such as desert temples, villages and trail ruins. These allow the player to explore the world and discover new things in different biomes.

There are many different play styles that minecraft is suitable for due to the customisabilty of different worlds. A player can play with or without monsters and there are many different game rules the player can toggle including keeping inventory after death and toggling fire spreading.

There is also a multiplayer aspect to minecraft, you can play with you friends using realms, servers or simply opening up your world to online play. This allows for an easy time playing with friends locally or otherwise.
